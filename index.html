About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Priscila Vargas Cruz. I am 25 years old and am an SF native. I am the daughter of immigrants, and I am extremely proud of that. My background has shaped who I am and who I will always be, and it motivates me to work hard and fight for what is right. My hobbies include Pilates, hanging out with my friends and boyfriend, being a foodie, hiking, and shopping. I love staying active but also making time for connection and opportunities to create memories. I am extremely passionate about travel. I feel extremely grateful for being fortunate enough to travel the world, so I fully intend on taking advantage of that in this lifetime! A core memory this summer was seeing elephants in Thailand and having ramen in Japan! The best part of traveling for me is immersing myself in the culture and meeting people from all over the globe along the way. Overall, I am someone who values culture, my loved ones, and creating a life that feels intentional and meaningful. I am excited for all the work I will be able to get done in this class, and I look forward to getting to know more about my classmates.
